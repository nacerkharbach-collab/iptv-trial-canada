Not always. Many Canadian ISPs work fine without a VPN. However, if you notice buffering or channels not opening, using a VPN with a Canadian or nearby location often stabilizes everything. We can guide you if needed.

What happens after the 24H ends?

Your test line expires automatically. No auto-renew and no surprise charges. If you want to continue, you simply buy a plan on our pricing page and we activate your subscription.

Can I use the trial on multiple devices?

The trial is designed for one main device so we can keep things stable for everyone. On paid plans you can choose packages that support more screens and devices.
